# Quantum Computing Journal

This is a journal that combines quantum computing learning materials, a roadmap, and weekly
logs of my progress. It is written in the spirit of "learning in public" and includes coding
examples, notes, and resources that I use throughout my journey.

As I continue learning, I will also document quantum computing concepts and attach links to 
any quantum-computing-related repositories or projects I create in the future.

## About Me

I completed my Master's degree in Physics and initially aimed to become a research scientist,
but things changed, and I am now working as a software developer.
Recently, I became fascinated by quantum computing because it allows me to combine my software
development skills with my interest in exploring and revisiting quantum physics concepts.

At first, I felt a bit rusty, so I created this repository to document my learning journey
and keep a record of what I learn and how I learn it. It serves as a personal reference and 
may also be useful for anyone interested in following along or exploring these topics in the future.
